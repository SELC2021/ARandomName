Overview

Asia is the largest continent in the world. It takes up 30% of the world's land mass. Asia also has a wide array of climates given it mass land form.

Iconic Landmarks

Asia is home to many proflic landmarks and landscapes. There are architectural feats such as the The Forbidden City( (the largest imperial palace in the world), the Great wall of China, and the Taj Mahal. Natural landscapes include the Mount Everest, the Dead Dea, and the Chocalate Hills.

Climate

AS mentioned earlier Asia has an extreme range of climates. There is polar climate up near the north yet inwards temperate climate can be seen. There is a big amount of rainforests under the polar climate.
