Australia

While Australia is one of the biggest countries, it is the smallest continent. As most of us already know, Australia is well known for its wildlife and animals. Animals such as kooalas, kangaroos, tasmanian devils, and emus. Zoos and Wildlife sancataries are common in Australia, so visiting Australian wildlife is something you should consider while on your visit to Australia.

The Melbourne Zoo

The Melbourne Zoo is a long running zoo in the city of Melbourne. The tickets are 38 dollars for adult, $19 for kids 4 to 15 and 34 for seniors. The zoo has been open since 1862 and offers over 5,000 animals.

Hours: Open Monday through Sunday 9AM to 5PM

Address: Elliott Ave, Melbourne, Victoria 3052

Phone Number: 1300 966 784

Website: https://zoo.org.au/
