Paris

Paris is the capital of France. Its has a population of around 2 million residents. Paris is known for its beatiful scenery and french cusuine. France experiences alot of tourism, especially during the summer - this is why it is cheaper to visit Paris during the Spring and Fall months.

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower that was build in 1887. You can purchase tickets onlines to see this iconic european peice of architecture. The Effiel tower also has multiple resturants around to visit such as the Bulle Parisienne and the Au Bon Accueil.
