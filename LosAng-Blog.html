Los Angeles

Los Angeles is the all american city. It is a city known for its diversity and ability to connect people. Los Angeles has a population of around 4 millon, making it the most populated city in California. Los Angeles normally sees relatively nice weather.

Universal Studios Hollywood

Universal Studios is a fun palce to visit espicially if you are looking for a nice family oriented travel experience.The studio is one of Hollywoods oldest standing filming studios. The studio offers fun activities and a theme park.

Hours: The studio is open Monday through Sunday from 10AM to 6PM.
