Seoul

Seoul is the capital of South Korea. It is a bustling town wit around 9 million people. The club scene in Seoul is alive and lively. Seoul is also full of cheap roadside street food vendors that offer a wide variety of authethic korean cuisine.

Gyeongdong Market

The Gyeongdong market is a located in the Dongdaemun district. It is also known as the Kyungdong Market. It is a market that specilizes in selling herbs medicine, and argicultural markets. The market is well known for its fruit and fish market.
